⠀Your score explained

Test takers at this band typically provide a thorough and well-structured response that fully addresses the question.

(AC) They present a clear and accurate overview, with relevant details that effectively summarize data.

(GT) The purpose of the letter is consistently clear, and the tone is appropriate for the context and audience.

Test takers express their point of view with well-developed ideas and relevant examples. Their writing is well-organized, with logical progression and effective use of cohesive devices. They use a wide range of vocabulary accurately, with only rare errors in word choice or spelling. Grammar is highly accurate, with a mix of complex and varied sentence structures that are mostly error-free. Punctuation is well-handled, with minimal mistakes that rarely interfere with the reader’s understanding.




⠀Advice to improve your score

Practice writing with a focus on clarity and depth. Begin by crafting a concise summary sentence that encapsulates the main points you intend to discuss. A strong opening sentence will guide the reader and provide a clear roadmap for your writing. Organize your points into distinct paragraphs, ensuring each one addresses a single idea for easier navigation.
